Privacy Policy

ByteTrim ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

ByteTrim does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

All image compression and processing take place entirely on your device.

Your photos are never uploaded to any server and never leave your device.

ByteTrim may request permission to access your photos or storage only to:

These permissions are used only for the features provided by the app.

Third-Party Services

ByteTrim does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cloud storage, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, collect, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

Children's Privacy

ByteTrim does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because all processing is performed locally on your device, your images remain under your control.
